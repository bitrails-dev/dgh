## Cross-Cutting Findings

### 1. Website Quality Maturity Model

| Level | Description | Hospitals |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| **Level 5 - Full Digital** | Online booking, patient portal, telemedicine, app, results, payments | Cleopatra Group, 57357 |
| **Level 4 - Advanced** | Online booking, detailed services, insurance lookup, contact forms | Saudi German, As-Salam, Dar Al Fouad |
| **Level 3 - Moderate** | Service listings, doctor directory, contact, basic booking | Shifa, IMC, Katameya, Andalusia, Magrabi, Watany Eye |
| **Level 2 - Basic** | Static pages, address/phone, limited service info | Farid Habib, Ibn Sina, Oasis, El-Sherouk, Tiba, Pearl, ABC |
| **Level 1 - Minimal** | Placeholder or single page | Most university hospitals, regional hospitals |
| **Level 0 - No Website** | No discoverable web presence | South Sinai, Pyramids Sharm, Hurghada public hospitals |
