# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- **Primary**: Egyptian general public — patients, their families, and community members seeking healthcare services.
- **Secondary**: Job seekers, researchers, and media interested in the hospital's achievements.
- **Context**: Users may be anxious, in health distress, or searching for a trusted institution. They need clarity, quick scanning, and a sense of absolute confidence.
- **Job to be done**: Evaluate the hospital's credibility, find departments/doctors, and access emergency/contact information — often on mobile, often under high stress.

## Product Purpose
Al Noor Hospital's digital presence serves as a beacon of medical excellence and human compassion. It exists to bridge the gap between world-class medical infrastructure and the community it serves, providing a seamless, dignified gateway to healthcare.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Authoritative but warm, formal but approachable.
- **Tone**: Calm, trustworthy, and professional.
- **3-word personality**: Trustworthy · Warm · Dignified
- **Emotional goal**: The visitor should feel: "This is a hospital I can trust with my family's lives."

## Anti-references
- **No "Startup" Fluff**: Avoid playful animations, neon gradients, or overly casual copy.
- **No Sterile Plainness**: Avoid the "cold clinic" look; the UI must feel human and inhabited.
- **No Trendy Minimalism**: Avoid excessive whitespace or ultra-thin typography that compromises readability for elderly or stressed users.

## Design Principles
1. **Dignified Bilingualism**: Arabic and English receive equal visual weight. Arabic typography is never an afterthought.
2. **Warm Authority**: Use deep Navy for competence and Gold/Teal for humanity. The UI should feel like a respected public institution.
3. **Clarity Over Decoration**: Every visual element must serve a functional purpose for a stressed user.
4. **Mobile-First Urgency**: Emergency access and contact points are always ≤2 taps away.
5. **Legacy of Excellence**: Seamlessly blend the 1950s founding legacy with 2026 digital-first healthcare.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Standard**: Targeting WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- **Low-Bandwidth Optimized**: Performance-first assets for users in varying connectivity zones.
- **Visual Clarity**: High contrast and robust font weights for accessibility under stress or visual impairment.
